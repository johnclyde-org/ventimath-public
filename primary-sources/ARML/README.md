# ARML - American Regions Mathematics League

## Contest Owner

**American Regions Mathematics League**
- Website: https://www.arml.com/

## About

ARML is an annual national mathematics competition for high school students in the United States. Teams from various regions compete in individual, team, and relay rounds.
