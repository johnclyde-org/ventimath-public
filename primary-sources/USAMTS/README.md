# USAMTS - USA Mathematical Talent Search

## Contest Owner

**Early Years**: Dr. George Berzsenyi (founder)
**Recent Years**: Art of Problem Solving
- Website: https://usamts.org/

## About

The USA Mathematical Talent Search (USAMTS) is a free mathematics competition open to all United States middle and high school students. Founded by Dr. George Berzsenyi, the competition is now run by Art of Problem Solving.
