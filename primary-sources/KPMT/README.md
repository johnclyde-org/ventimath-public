# KPMT - Knights of Pi Math Tournament

## Contest Owner

**Newport High School Math Club**
- Location: Bellevue, Washington

## About

The Knights of Pi Math Tournament (KPMT) is a student-run math competition hosted by Newport High School in Bellevue, Washington.
